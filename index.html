What's the contract for?

This RFP will address the City of Pittsburgh’s need for regular, efficient and effective custodial and cleaning services for the 22 recreation and Healthy Active Living (senior) centers specified in this document. These facilities, ranging from 2,000 – 15,000 sq. ft. have basic custodial needs that must be addressed multiple times each week by a custodial contractor. Regular custodial duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Floor mopping sweeping
  • Carpet vacuuming
  • Equipment and furnishing dusting
  • Trash collection mitigation, including collection and removal
  • Disinfecting of equipment, kitchens, eating areas and restrooms
  • Window and mirror washing
  • Litter picking
  • Dispenser replenishing
  • Wipe down of desks, tables, equipment and furniture
  • Light bulb replacement
  • Other light duties, as assigned

What about supplies?
All cleaning supplies will be provided by Citiparks, including trash bags, cleaning solutions, mops, brooms, rags, soap, lightbulbs and paper products.
How long is the contract term?
The term of any agreement will be between one and three years.

What will I need to submit?
Details will be provided when the RFP is officially announced, but here's a brief rundown:
  • Letter of Transmittal: A statement indicating your understanding of the work to be performed, your qualifications, the name of the contact person at your company and where they can be reached, and a statement of any potential relationships with City employees.
  • Profile of Respondent: Should be a brief statement indicating the Respondent’s experience in conducting work of the nature sought by this RFP. Advertising brochures of the Respondent may be submitted as a part of this profile as long as the brochures specifically address the experience of the Respondent related to the work to be performed. Additionally, this profile should include the location of the office service will be provided from as well as any other information that relates to the capabilities of your company to do the required work.
  • Explanation of Work to be Performed: A detailed description of the methods and procedures you will use to do the required work.
  • Cost: Should be based on the hours of work provided, not including travel.
  • Other: Non-Discrimination Certification (Appendix A); MBE, WBE & Veteran-Owned Solicitation and Commitment Form (Appendix B)
How will my response be evaluated?
Again, more details will be provided with the RFP, but here's the evaluation breakdown:
  • Technical Expertise & Experience: 20%
  • Procedures and Methods: 15%
  • Cost: 50%
  • Location: 5%
  • Past Work Experience: 10%
What about insurance requirements?
The standard insurance coverage required by the City of Pittsburgh for professional services contracts is as follows:
  • All insurance must be on an occurrence basis, not a claims-made basis
  • The City must be listed as an additional named insured, not merely as a certificate holder
  • Insurance should provide 30 days cancellation notice
  • The Respondent shall provide an insurance certificate showing that it meets the requirements
  • Worker’s Compensation shall meet statutory requirements
  • General liability (including property damage and bodily injury), automobile liability and professional liability shall be provided in the following amounts:
    • Public liability and property damage
    Bodily injury, including death and property $500,000.00 per occurrence
    Damage combined $1,000,000.00 aggregate
    Automobile Liability and Property Damage
    Bodily injury, including death and property $500,000.00 per occurrence
    Damage combined $1,000,000.00 aggregate
